Can I change or cancel my trip?

Absolutely. You can reschedule or cancel free of charge up to 7 days before departure, no questions asked.

Is travel insurance really included?

Yes — every plan comes with full medical and trip-cancellation insurance covering the whole duration of your journey.

Do I need previous trekking experience?

Not at all. The Explorer plan is beginner-friendly, and our guides adapt the pace to the group on every route.

What's your group size?

It depends on the plan — from intimate groups of 6 on the Adventurer plan to fully private trips on Voyager.
