Privacy Policy

Home / Privacy policy

Last updated: 1 January 2026

At GoTrip, your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website and services you agree to the practices described below.

Information we collect

  • Account details you provide, such as your name and email address.
  • Booking information needed to plan and deliver your trips.
  • Technical data like your browser type and pages visited, collected automatically.

How we use your information

  • To create your account and process your bookings.
  • To send confirmations, updates, and (if you opt in) our newsletter.
  • To improve our services and keep the website secure.

Sharing your information

We never sell your personal data. We only share it with trusted partners who help us deliver your trips (for example, local guides and payment processors), and only to the extent needed to provide the service.

Your rights

You can access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. You may also unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link in any message. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@gotrip.com.

Cookies

We use cookies to remember your preferences and understand how the site is used. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Reach out via our Contact page or email hello@gotrip.com.
