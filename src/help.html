Support

How can we help?

Find quick answers below, or reach out and our team will get back to you within a few hours.

How do I book a trip?

Pick a plan on the Pricing page, create an account, and follow the checkout steps. You'll get a confirmation email with all the details.

Can I reschedule or get a refund?

Yes — you can reschedule or cancel free of charge up to 7 days before departure. Refunds are processed within 5 business days.

What should I bring on a trip?

After booking we send a personalised packing list. Premium plans include most of the gear, so you usually just need clothing and personal items.

How do I contact support?

Use the Contact page, email hello@gotrip.com, or call +1 800 123 45 67 — we're available 24/7.
